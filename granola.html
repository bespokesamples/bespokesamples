granola

granular texturizer.

Hear Granola in action!

Just 7 knobs and instant results.

  1. Position – Where in the audio the grains are taken from (ms)
  2. Size – How long each grain is (ms)
  3. Interval – Time between grains (ms)
  4. Pitch – Shifts grain pitch up or down (st)
  5. Width – Stereo spread of grains (%)
  6. Mix – Balance between dry and wet signal (%)
  7. Gain – Output volume (%)
